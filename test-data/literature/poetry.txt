Poetry is a form of literature that uses aesthetic and often rhythmic qualities of language.
Poetry has a long and varied history throughout the world.
Poetry often uses figurative language, imagery, and symbolism to convey emotion.
Ancient poetry was often sung or recited aloud as part of oral tradition.
Modern poetry takes many forms including free verse, haiku, and sonnets.
Language in poetry is used in ways that differ from ordinary prose.
Poetry can be found in every culture and language throughout human history.
The language of poetry is carefully chosen for its sound and meaning.
