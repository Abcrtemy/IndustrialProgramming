Physics is the natural science that studies matter, its fundamental constituents, its motion and behavior through space and time.
Physics is one of the most fundamental scientific disciplines.
The goal of physics is to understand how the universe behaves.
Physics is one of the oldest academic disciplines and through its inclusion of astronomy, perhaps the oldest.
Energy, force, and motion are central concepts in physics.
Quantum physics describes the behavior of matter and energy at the smallest scales.
Classical physics covers Newton's laws of motion and thermodynamics.
Modern physics includes relativity and quantum mechanics.
